Introduction

What is FinEats?

FinEats is a smart food budget tracker designed for everyday Filipinos. Log your meals, track your spending, and discover affordable alternatives — all in one place.

Whether you're a student on a tight budget, a professional watching your expenses, or a family looking to save — FinEats is built for you.
✓ Set daily food budgets in seconds
✓ Log meals from carenderia to fast food
✓ Get smart suggestions when over budget
✓ Earn PayBack Points with every meal
